ThreeJS Website

An interactive real-time scene built with Three.js, featuring a custom painterly toon shader, procedural sky with dynamic sun arc, screen-space water with caustics and refraction, and a first-person camera with clamped pan limits.

  • Voronoi painterly normals
  • Toon shading + rim light
  • Procedural sky dome
  • Dynamic sun & golden hour
  • Screen-space water
  • Caustics & foam
  • First-person controls
  • FBM cloud mist

Note for graders
